Your Family Basecamp for Hocking Hills

Cave and Creek is a fully furnished 4-bedroom cabin tucked into 20 quiet acres of Ohio woodland. You're remote enough to hear the breeze and the owls at night — yet only a one-mile drive to Old Man's Cave, the most-loved trail in Hocking Hills State Park. Bring the kids, bring the grandparents, bring the hiking boots. We built this place for families who want to spend their days outside and their evenings around the fire.

Explore the Property

Outdoors

Step outside to three mowed hilltop acres with sweeping views of the surrounding forest. Soak in the private hot tub under the stars, gather around the fire pit for s'mores, grill out on the spacious deck, or unwind on the screened-in porch with your morning coffee — this is where your best vacation memories happen.

First Floor

The heart of the cabin. A sun-filled living room with vaulted ceilings flows into a fully equipped kitchen — perfect for family dinners after a long day on the trails. A cozy queen bedroom and a kids' favorite bunk room with two sets of bunks make this floor feel like home from the moment you walk in.

Top Floor

Your private retreat above it all. Climb upstairs to a spacious king bedroom with its own en suite bathroom — a quiet escape where you can recharge while the kids play downstairs.

Walk Out Basement

The ultimate hangout spot. A carpeted bar area with a pull-out couch is ideal for movie nights or a game of cards after dark. Down the hall, a king bedroom with its own en suite bathroom offers a second private suite — and the walk-out door puts you steps from the yard and hot tub.

Please note: Cave and Creek is a smoke-free, pet-free home so all of our guests can enjoy a clean, allergen-free stay.

The Area — Hocking Hills, Ohio

Cave and Creek sits on 20 private acres — three mowed acres surrounding the cabin and seventeen acres of mature Ohio woods to explore. It's remote enough to feel like a true escape, but the most popular spots in Hocking Hills are minutes away.

Old Man's Cave is one mile down the road. Hocking Hills State Park Lodge is just a couple of miles further. You can be on a trailhead inside of five minutes from the front door.
